# Privacy Policy for Florra

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Florra is a note-taking and planning application published under the Saii brand.
This Privacy Policy explains what data the app may process, what data is not collected, and how information is handled.

## Information We Collect

Florra may collect limited anonymous analytics data to help improve app quality and stability.
This may include:

- App session duration
- Feature usage frequency
- Crash reports
- Basic diagnostic and performance information

## Information We Do Not Collect

Florra does not collect:

- The content of your notes
- Your voice memo content
- Personally identifying profile information for app use
- Device identifiers when analytics collection is disabled

## How Your Data Is Stored

Your notes and app content are stored locally on your device.
Florra is designed so that your written content remains on-device.

## Analytics

If analytics is enabled, anonymous usage information may be processed through Google Analytics or similar analytics tooling to help improve the app experience.
This information is used for:

- Understanding app performance
- Identifying crashes and stability issues
- Improving features and usability

Analytics data is not used to read, store, or profile the content of your notes.

## Third-Party Services

Florra may rely on third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, infrastructure, or app distribution, including services provided by Apple and Google.
These services may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Florra is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can disable analytics in the app settings if that option is available in your version of the app.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
